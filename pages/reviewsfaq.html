Generally Asked Questions

How Should Cakes/ Cupcakes be Stored?

All cakes and cupcakes should be stored in the refridgerator then chilled up to thirty minutes before being served. The best method to keep them tasting fresh and lasting longer is keeping them stored in the refridgerator until the point in which thy are being consumed!

How Far in Advance Should I Order?

Orders should be placed at least one week in advance from their requested pick-up date.

What if What I Want is Not on the Order Form?

Inquires about items or options not listed on the Order Form can be made through email. Responses will be made during business pick-up hours. Please allow for at least one hour in response time.

What Payment Methods are Accepted?

Cashapp and Zelle are currently the only methods of accepted payment at the time.

What Options are there for Delivery?

We currently allow for deliveries through Uber Connect as well as direct deliveries which will cost an extra fee, which is doenedent upon the size and quantity of goods being delievered.

How can I Request Delivery?

Delivery can be requested by email or through contacting the phone number provided.

Pickup Hours are Everyday between 1 PM - 8 PM.
